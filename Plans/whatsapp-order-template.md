# WhatsApp Order Template – Areesha's Collection

**Created:** 2026-03-01
**Purpose:** Standardize the WhatsApp order-taking process

---

## STEP 1: Customer Inquiry

When a customer messages, reply with:

> Assalam o Alaikum! 🤍
> Areesha's Collection mein aapka swagat hai!
>
> Aap konsi piece mein interested hain? Photo ya name bhej dein — main details bhej deti hoon! 🛍️

---

## STEP 2: Product Details Reply

> ✨ [Product Name]
>
> 💰 Price: Rs. [Price]
> 📏 Available sizes: [S / M / L / XL / Free size]
> 🧵 Fabric: [Fabric type]
> 📦 Type: [Stitched / Unstitched]
>
> 🚚 Delivery: [X] working days
> 💳 Payment: COD / Bank Transfer / JazzCash / EasyPaisa
>
> Order confirm karein? 👇

---

## STEP 3: Order Confirmation

When customer says yes, ask:

> Perfect choice! 🤍 Order confirm karne ke liye yeh details bhejein:
>
> 1️⃣ Full Name:
> 2️⃣ Complete Address (with city):
> 3️⃣ Phone Number:
> 4️⃣ Item(s): [Product name + size]
> 5️⃣ Payment Method: COD / Bank Transfer / JazzCash / EasyPaisa

---

## STEP 4: Order Confirmation Reply

After receiving details:

> ✅ ORDER CONFIRMED!
>
> 📋 Order Details:
> 👤 Name: [Name]
> 📍 Address: [Address]
> 📱 Phone: [Number]
> 🛍️ Item(s): [Product + size]
> 💰 Total: Rs. [Amount]
> 💳 Payment: [Method]
> 🚚 Estimated Delivery: [Date]
>
> Shukriya! Aapka order process ho raha hai 🤍
> Hum aapko tracking details bhej dein ge!

---

## STEP 5: Shipping Update

> 📦 Shipping Update!
>
> Aapka order dispatch ho gaya hai! 🚚
>
> 🔢 Tracking #: [Number]
> 📅 Expected delivery: [Date]
>
> Koi sawal ho toh message karein! 🤍

---

## STEP 6: Post-Delivery Follow-up

> Assalam o Alaikum! 🤍
>
> Aapka order mil gaya? Umeed hai aapko pasand aaya!
>
> Agar aapko acha laga toh:
> ⭐ Review de dein — hum aapko feature karein ge!
> 📸 Photo bhejein — hamari page pe share karein ge!
>
> Shukriya for choosing Areesha's Collection 🤍
