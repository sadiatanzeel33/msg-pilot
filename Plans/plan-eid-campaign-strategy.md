## Content Pillars

1. **Product Showcase** — high-quality photos, flat-lays, detail shots
2. **Styling Inspiration** — how to wear, color combos, accessory pairing
3. **Urgency & Scarcity** — limited stock, deadline reminders
4. **Social Proof** — reviews, customer photos, testimonials
5. **Brand Story** — behind the scenes, craftsmanship, Areesha's journey

---

## WhatsApp Strategy

- **Broadcast List:** Segment into (a) previous buyers (b) inquiries (c) new leads
- **Order Template:** Standard message with item name, size, price, delivery date
- **Status Updates:** Minimum 2x/week with new arrivals & offers
- **Quick Replies:** Pre-written responses for common questions (price, size, delivery)

## Risk Analysis
- **Stock running out early** → Keep buffer stock for best sellers
- **Delivery delays** → Set clear last-order dates, communicate early
- **Low engagement** → Boost top-performing posts with small paid budget (requires Gold tier approval)
- **Customer complaints** → Respond within 2 hours on all platforms
